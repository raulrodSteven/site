Short Biography

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Dynamical Systems and Ergodic Theory at the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio), under the guidance of Lorenzo J. Díaz.

Currently, I am a visiting student (sandwich doctorate) at the Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP) in Trieste, Italy, under the supervision of Stefano Luzzatto.

Previously, I completed my Master's degree at PUC-Rio, studying the statistical behavior of skew products and Schwarzian derivatives.
