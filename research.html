Raul R. Chavez

Research Description

My research interests lie in the broad area of Dynamical Systems and Ergodic Theory. I am particularly interested in the study of statistical properties of partially hyperbolic systems and the mechanisms underlying bifurcation theory.

Currently, my work focuses on investigating the approximation of non-hyperbolic measures and understanding the interplay between topological and statistical behaviors in such contexts.
